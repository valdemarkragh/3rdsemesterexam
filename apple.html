Apple

At Apple we employ millions of people around the world. The decisions we make can have a big impact on their lives and their communities. We have a great responsibility to protect the rights of all the people in our supply chain, and to do everything we can to preserve our planet's fragile environment.

Supporting Local Communites

‘Global Volunteer Program’ was launched in 2011 to encourage employees to volunteer in local communities. Since its launch more than USD 78 million was donated to charities and non-profits around the world

Labour and Human Rights

Apple has investigated cases of abuses of foreign workers and the company has required suppliers to reimburse affected “foreign contract workers USD3.9 million in excessive fees paid to labour brokers, bringing our total reimbursements since 2008 to USD16.9 million”

Employee Health and Safety

The company has launched Apple Supplier EHS Academy, an 18-month program that aims to improve employee health and safety in the industry throughout the globe. 240 suppliers and 270,000 workers have participated in this program..
